Beginner's Guide to Healthy Skin ✨

Created by Pranaya Bollu

ISLT 4361 - Introduction to Digital Media
Summer 2026

Welcome to this interactive learning module! This lesson introduces beginners to a simple skincare routine using six essential products. Learners will understand the purpose of each product, the correct order of application, and how skincare steps work together.

🎯 Student Learning Objectives

  • Identify the six essential products used in a beginner skincare routine.
  • Explain the purpose of each skincare product.
  • Apply skincare products in the correct order.
  • Create an independent beginner skincare routine.

🌸 Learning Infographic

Created by me: This infographic was designed using Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Firefly. The visual organization, icons, and color choices were created to simplify skincare concepts and help learners understand the routine sequence.

🧴 Six Steps to a Simple Skincare Routine

1. Cleanser 🫧

CeraVe Foaming Facial Cleanser
Removes dirt, oil, makeup, and impurities from the skin.

2. Toner 💧

Byoma Hydrating Milky Toner
Hydrates the skin and prepares it for additional products.

3. Serum ✨

Glow Recipe Niacinamide Dew Drops
Targets skincare concerns and improves skin appearance.

4. Moisturizer 🧴

COSRX Advanced Snail Cream
Locks in hydration and supports the skin barrier.

5. Sunscreen ☀️

Supergoop SPF 30
Protects skin from harmful UV rays.

6. Lip Treatment 💋

OLEHENRIKSEN Pout Preserve
Hydrates, smooths, and protects dry lips.

💻 Digital Media Principles Applied

🖼 Visual Design

The infographic uses visual hierarchy, consistent colors, icons, and organized sections to reduce cognitive load and make information easier to understand.

🎥 Multimedia Learning

The instructional video combines visuals and narration so learners can see the skincare process while hearing explanations.

🎧 Audio Learning

The audio summary reinforces important information and provides another way for learners to review the lesson.

🎨 Consistency

The website uses consistent typography, colors, spacing, and layout to create a clear and engaging learning experience.

🌱 Learning Activities

Activity 1: Explore the Infographic

Learners identify the six skincare products and analyze the correct order using the visual infographic.

Activity 2: Watch the Skincare Video

Learners observe the skincare routine demonstration and connect visual examples with product explanations.

Activity 3: Complete the Knowledge Check

Learners complete the quiz to test their understanding and receive immediate feedback through their final score.

✨ How Digital Media Supports Learning Outcomes

The infographic, video, audio, and quiz work together to create a complete learning experience. The infographic introduces the skincare concepts visually, the video demonstrates the application process, and the audio reinforces key information through narration. The quiz allows learners to apply their knowledge and measure their understanding. Together, these digital media components support the learning objectives by helping students identify skincare products, understand their purposes, and independently create a beginner skincare routine.

🌸 Reflection Activity

After completing this lesson, reflect on the following questions:

1. Which skincare product do you think is the most important and why?
2. How would you teach this skincare routine to someone who has never used skincare products before?
