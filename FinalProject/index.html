💻 Digital Media Principles Applied

🖼️ Visual Design Principles

The skincare infographic uses visual hierarchy, consistent colors, icons, and organized sections to reduce cognitive load. Important information is grouped together so learners can quickly understand the order of the skincare routine.

🎥 Multimedia Learning Principle

The instructional video combines visuals and narration to demonstrate each skincare step. Learners can see the process while hearing explanations, which supports deeper understanding and knowledge retention.

🎧 Audio Learning Support

The audio summary provides an additional way for learners to review the content. It supports auditory learning and allows students to reinforce important skincare concepts outside of the visual activity.

🎨 Consistency and Design

The website uses a consistent color palette, typography, spacing, and layout. This creates a clear and engaging learning environment that allows students to focus on the content.

🌱 Interactive Learning Activities

Activity 1: Explore the Skincare Infographic

Learners examine the infographic and identify the six skincare products. They use the visual information to understand the correct order and purpose of each product.

Activity 2: Watch the Instructional Video

Learners watch the skincare demonstration video to observe how each product is applied. The video connects visual demonstrations with explanations to support practical understanding.

Activity 3: Complete the Knowledge Check

After reviewing the multimedia materials, learners complete the quiz to test their understanding of skincare steps and product purposes. The score provides immediate feedback about their learning.

✨ How Digital Media Supports Learning Outcomes

The digital media elements work together to create a complete learning experience. The infographic introduces the skincare concepts visually, the instructional video demonstrates how the routine is completed, and the audio summary reinforces key information through narration. The quiz allows learners to apply their knowledge and receive feedback. Together, these multimedia components support the learning objectives by helping students identify skincare products, understand their purposes, and apply the correct skincare routine independently.
