Career Reflection

My journey as an Information Technology student has helped me develop technical skills, professional confidence, and a better understanding of my career goals. Through my coursework, projects, and leadership experiences, I have gained experience with web development, programming, databases, networking, and problem-solving. Building this portfolio has allowed me to combine my technical abilities with creativity and design while showcasing my growth as an IT professional.

My work experiences and involvement in student organizations have also shaped my professional development. As a Coding Instructor at Code Ninjas, I learned how to communicate technical concepts clearly, support others, and become a stronger leader. My involvement with the Asian American Association and Campus Activities Programming Board has helped me improve my teamwork, event planning, organization, and communication skills.

Throughout my academic career, I have discovered my interest in creating technology solutions that are useful, accessible, and user-friendly. My career goal is to continue developing my skills in areas such as web development, software development, cybersecurity, and user experience design. I want to work in an environment where I can continue learning while contributing innovative ideas and solutions.

Looking toward the future, I hope to become a successful IT professional who combines technical knowledge with creativity and collaboration. The experiences I have gained through classes, projects, leadership roles, and employment have prepared me for future opportunities in the technology industry. I plan to continue expanding my skills, exploring new technologies, and creating digital experiences that positively impact users and organizations.
