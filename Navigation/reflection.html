Career Reflection

Throughout my Information Technology journey, I have developed a stronger understanding of technology, problem-solving, and professional growth. My coursework and experiences have allowed me to explore web development, programming, databases, cybersecurity, and user experience design. Creating this portfolio has helped me showcase my skills while reflecting on how technology can be used to create meaningful solutions.

My professional experiences have played an important role in shaping my career goals. Working as a Coding Instructor at Code Ninjas helped me improve my communication and leadership skills by teaching programming concepts to students. My involvement in organizations such as the Asian American Association and Campus Activities Programming Board has also strengthened my teamwork, event planning, and project management abilities.

As I continue my Information Technology education, my goal is to build a career where I can combine technical skills with creativity and problem solving. I am interested in opportunities involving web development, software solutions, cybersecurity, and user-focused technology. I want to continue learning new tools and technologies that will allow me to grow as an IT professional.

Looking ahead, I hope to become a technology professional who creates innovative and accessible digital experiences. This portfolio represents my growth as a student and demonstrates the skills I have gained through coursework, projects, and leadership experiences. I plan to continue improving my technical abilities while developing the communication and collaboration skills needed for success in the technology industry.
